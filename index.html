Organic Search

SEO and GEO, each with dedicated experts.

SEO gets your brand to the top of Google. GEO gets your brand mentioned inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. Two distinct disciplines, two specialist teams, one strategist making sure they reinforce each other.

How they work together

Strong SEO is what makes brand mentions possible.

LLMs don't pull from random sources. They reach for content that ranks well, has clean structure, and shows the kind of authority signals search engines have been rewarding for years.

That means the work that helps you rank on Google is also the work that helps you get mentioned by ChatGPT. Topical authority, entity clarity, structured data, internal linking, content depth. Same foundation, two payoffs.

So we don't pick a side. We build the foundation once and let it work for both channels.

01
The foundation
SEO.

The SEO work that earns rankings, and also earns mentions.

Modern SEO is built around entities, topical authority, structural signals, and a healthy backlink profile. We do the foundation work that helps your pages rank on Google and gives LLMs a clear, trustworthy picture of who you are and what you do.

  • Topical authority mapping
  • Entity and schema optimisation
  • Buyer intent keyword strategy
  • Programmatic SEO at scale
  • Backlink building and digital PR
  • Content audits and pruning
  • Information architecture
  • Technical SEO and Core Web Vitals
02
The new surface
GEO.

The work that gets your brand named inside AI tools.

Generative Engine Optimisation is the practice of making your brand citable and retrievable inside LLMs. We track where you currently get mentioned, find the prompts that drive your category, and build the content and signals that earn those mentions.

  • AI brand mention tracking
  • llms.txt and retrieval signals
  • Citation engineered content
  • Prompt driven keyword discovery
  • Entity disambiguation
  • Comparison and listicle plays
  • AEO for featured Google answers
  • LLM source set auditing
How we deliver it

Specialists on both sides. One strategist holding it together.

You get a dedicated SEO team and a dedicated GEO team, not generalists splitting their attention. A strategist sits across both, makes sure the content, schema, and link work serves both channels, and owns the single strategy you signed up for. Specialist depth, joined-up thinking.
